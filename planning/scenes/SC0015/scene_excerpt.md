INT. ROMAN'S STUDY — VALE RESIDENCE — NIGHT

A working room. Books that have been read, not arranged. A desk that is used. Roman is behind it. Nadia is in the chair across from it — the chair that people sit in when Roman is explaining something to them.

He has a tablet. He sets it face-down.

ROMAN
I want to explain the structure of the event. I'll be direct, and I'd ask that you listen completely before responding.

Nadia says nothing. He takes this as assent.

ROMAN (CONT'D)
The platform is called ORACLE PRIME. It operates as a closed betting network — subscription access, globally distributed, roughly eleven thousand active members. They don't know each other. They know the platform.

He pauses to let the number land. Not for effect — it is simply a pause he has learned is useful.

ROMAN (CONT'D)
The event is mediated by an automated broadcast system. A persona — it calls itself The Herald. It narrates and contextualizes what the audience observes. The audience receives curated feeds; The Herald provides framing and maintains the betting interface.

NADIA
What are they betting on.

ROMAN
You. Your decisions. Your movements. Outcomes in your life that the audience is given enough information to forecast.

A beat.

ROMAN (CONT'D)
The Herald has a technical constraint worth noting. It cannot fabricate live data. It reports what it receives from the underlying feed. When it quotes odds, those odds are real — they reflect what the audience is actually wagering. The system was designed that way. It's considered a quality guarantee.

He says this as someone who genuinely appreciates the elegance of it.

ROMAN (CONT'D)
The event has a defined duration. It runs until a terminal condition is reached. The terminal conditions are known to the audience and will be known to you.

NADIA
What are the terminal conditions.

ROMAN
Three. The event concludes if you exit the territory we've designated — certain, immediate. It concludes if you take an action that closes the audience's interest — the system determines this algorithmically, based on engagement metrics. And it concludes on a calendar endpoint, which I'm not disclosing at this time.

He opens a drawer. Removes a single folded card and sets it on the desk between them.

ROMAN (CONT'D)
The technical architecture includes a regulatory shutdown provision — a network emergency protocol built into the broadcast layer for compliance purposes. It's never been used. It exists because the licensing framework requires it.

He slides the card toward her. She does not take it.

ROMAN (CONT'D)
That's the contact information for the event's oversight layer. You won't need it. I'm providing it because the game is cleanest when both parties have equivalent documentation.

Nadia looks at him.

NADIA
Is Jin safe.

ROMAN
For as long as the event runs.

Silence.

She sits with this. She is not performing stillness — she is actually still, processing the architecture of the four words. Duration. Contingency. Participation. Return.

She does not cry.

She does not ask anything else.

She picks up the card.

ROMAN (CONT'D)
You're free to move within the designated territory. You'll be observed. You already know that.

NADIA
When does it start.

ROMAN
It started this morning.

He stands. She does not.

ROMAN (CONT'D)
You have more resources than you know. I find that the event is more interesting when participants understand that.

He leaves her with the card and the quiet study and the full weight of knowing exactly what she is in.
