INT. KASPAR TERMINAL — SERVICE CORRIDOR — NIGHT

The fever broke two hours ago. Nadia knows this because she checked the temperature three times afterward. Thirty-seven point two. Not optimal. Functional.

She hears the step before she sees the woman.

The woman who enters through the east access gate uses a keycard — supply worker channels. She has done preparation. She is slight, moves with the economy of someone who has learned to make herself small in order to think without interruption. Courier bag held against her body. She stops when she sees Nadia.

Nadia does not move.

CHEN MEI
Nadia Vale.

Not a question.

NADIA
You have ten seconds.

CHEN MEI
Chen Mei. Sera Vas opened a channel eighteen hours ago. I've been in transit for sixteen of those hours. I —

She stops. Repeats the phrase quietly, as if auditing the output.

CHEN MEI (CONT'D)
Sixteen of those hours.

NADIA
Who sent you.

CHEN MEI
No one sent me. I came because Marcus told me —

Her hands move briefly over the courier bag. Small sorting motions.

CHEN MEI (CONT'D)
I need you to understand what this is before I give it to you. That was — he said: she will not take something she doesn't understand. He said that. About you specifically.

Nadia runs the sentence back.

NADIA
What else did he say.

Chen Mei's eyes go glassy. She blinks rapidly, twice.

CHEN MEI
That you would ask that.

A beat.

NADIA
There's a camera above the north bay door. You're inside the broadcast zone.

CHEN MEI
I know. I calculated —

A pause.

CHEN MEI (CONT'D)
I calculated it was worth it.

NADIA
What's in the bag.

CHEN MEI
A copy of a ledger. Dimitri's. Marcus built the chain before he was killed. The transfer protocol requires a physical copy. I've been carrying it for eleven days. I confirmed the timestamps twice.

She adds, quietly.

CHEN MEI (CONT'D)
Twice.

Nadia studies her. The woman is very still — not naturally still, effortfully so.

NADIA
Dimitri's.

CHEN MEI
Yes.

NADIA
He said I wouldn't take something I didn't understand.

CHEN MEI
Yes.

NADIA
He was right.

A beat.

NADIA (CONT'D)
Come inside.

Chen Mei crosses the threshold. The camera above the north bay door has a record of her arrival. It does not have a record of what passed between them in the service corridor. That exchange happened out of frame, which is either lucky or not, depending on who is watching.
