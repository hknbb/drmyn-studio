INT. VELTAIN — ROMAN'S OPERATIONS CENTER — NIGHT

The same long room. The display wall is running the post-event analytics from the MERIN sequence: coverage maps, hunter coordination overlays, the final tracking gap where the subject exited the service district infrastructure and dropped below confirmed sensor coverage.

The numbers running along the bottom of the display are not operational. They are financial. Betting volume figures from the broadcast's MERIN coverage window. They are extraordinary.

Roman stands at the display. He has been reading it for some time.

Dimitri is at the work table. He has a tablet and a bound ledger beside it — a small, dark notebook, unremarkable. He has kept it throughout. He is reviewing his own document before presenting it.

He brings the tablet to Roman.

DIMITRI
Post-sequence exposure assessment. MERIN outcome.

Roman takes the tablet. He reads. He does not scan — he reads the entire document before responding, which is his practice.

The document is organized. No editorial. Four sections: operational status, exposure variables, risk trajectory, recommended resolution. Under recommended resolution, a single line: managed termination, structured payout, standard protocol.

Roman finishes. He returns the tablet.

He looks at the display wall — specifically at the tracking gap. The space where the subject stopped being visible.

ROMAN
The MERIN sequence drove the highest single-minute betting volume
this event has recorded.

He is not answering the document. He is establishing the frame.

ROMAN (CONT'D)
The close call added eighteen percent to aggregate engagement
across all active audience tiers. The Herald's post-sequence
analysis has already run. Our audience is invested.

DIMITRI
The subject is outside confirmed sensor coverage. No tracker
contact in four hours and eleven minutes. Compound fracture
risk is rated significant by the field medical assessment. She
has lost her primary operational contact. Her resource position
is minimal.

He is not arguing. He is adding data.

DIMITRI (CONT'D)
These are optimal termination conditions. A managed resolution
in the next twelve-hour window is achievable and clean.

Roman turns from the display.

ROMAN
Understand — what you're pricing as exposure is the event's
current value proposition.

He does not rush this.

ROMAN (CONT'D)
An asset who cannot be tracked, who has survived conditions
that should have resolved the event three times over, who
is physically degraded but operationally unpredictable. That
is not a risk variable. That is a broadcast asset. The audience
is watching because they do not know how this resolves.

DIMITRI
Her behavioral profile is outside modeled parameters. The
intelligence acquisition in the SEQ05 period indicates she
has information about the event architecture we have not
confirmed the scope of.

ROMAN
She has partial intelligence about a system she does not have
the resources to use. She is injured, alone, and in a port
city with no confirmed allies. The event resolves in our favor.

He says this with the calm of a man who has priced it correctly and is not interested in re-pricing it.

ROMAN (CONT'D)
The event continues.

A pause.

Dimitri does not push. There is nowhere to push. He has presented the analysis. The analysis has been received and declined. This is the complete structure of the exchange.

He reaches to the work table. He picks up the ledger.

He opens it to the current page — the entries are small, precise, dated. He uncaps his pen.

He writes. The entry takes approximately twelve seconds. He caps the pen. He closes the ledger.

Roman has seen this. He has seen Dimitri make entries before — the ledger is a known instrument, a record of operational decisions. He has never asked what it contains.

He does not ask now.

DIMITRI
Confirmed.

He sets the tablet on the work table. He picks up the ledger.

The display wall continues to run its analytics. The betting volume figures scroll along the bottom: the MERIN sequence numbers, then the pre-MERIN baseline, then the projected next-session figures, which are higher.

Roman turns back to the display.

The gap in the tracking data — the service district exit corridor — is still there.

He looks at it for a moment.

He picks up the glass near his hand and sets it down again, a small precise movement, without looking at it.

The room is quiet.
