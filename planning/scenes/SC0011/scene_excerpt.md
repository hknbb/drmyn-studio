```fountain
ROMAN places a document on the table between them. Not dramatically — the way you place something you have been carrying and are ready to set down.

He does not speak.

Nadia looks at the document. The laboratory letterhead. The reference number. The dates — she recognizes the dates; she was there. The name at the top is not hers.

She looks at it for a measured time. Long enough to read it; not long enough to suggest she is affected by reading it.

She looks up.

ROMAN
I had it confirmed by two independent sources. The laboratory, and a private medical consultant who examined the same material.

A pause.

ROMAN (CONT'D)
I want to understand your thinking.

He says this with genuine interest. He is not performing curiosity. He actually wants to know how she modeled the risk.

NADIA
What specifically would you like to understand.

ROMAN
The decision to continue. After Jin was born.

She looks at him. The composure is intact. It is costing her a great deal and he cannot see that.

NADIA
The affair was real. Marcus was the father. I'm not going to tell you otherwise.

ROMAN
No.

NADIA
I calculated that confirming it was more useful than denying it. A denial you could disprove is a position. A confirmation is —

ROMAN
I know what you calculated.

The room is quiet.

ROMAN (CONT'D)
I've been doing this longer than you've been married to me.

He says this without cruelty. It is a statement of fact about relative experience in a specific field.

NADIA
Then you know the confirmation is accurate.

ROMAN
Yes.

He nods once — the acknowledgment of a variable that has been priced and is now closed.

He looks past her toward the door.

---

The door opens.

DIMITRI enters from the corridor. Behind him, two men. One of them is carrying JIN — eight months old, alert, looking at the ceiling with the focused attention of a person who has found something worth examining.

Nadia does not move.

She watches Jin pass. As he crosses in front of her, she sees his wrist — the pale blue hospital bracelet, the clasp she adjusted that morning, his name in small type. It is at the height of her eyeline as he is carried past.

She does not reach for him. She does not stand. Her hands are flat on the table in front of her.

The door closes.

ROMAN
You'll be given appropriate time to prepare.

He stands. Straightens his jacket.

ROMAN (CONT'D)
The documentation is yours to keep.

He leaves.

Dimitri leaves.

---

NADIA sits at the long table in the formal room.

The document is in front of her.

Her hands have not moved.

She is looking at the door.
```
