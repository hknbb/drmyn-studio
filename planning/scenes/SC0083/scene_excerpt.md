The drainage access is forty meters north of the junction, set into the base of a freight loading bay. Steel grating, flush with the pavement. She saw it on the transit infrastructure map she memorized from the deployment documents' coverage annotations.

She pulls it. It opens. She drops through in one motion, catches the inner ladder, pulls the grating closed above her.

The footsteps arrive. Pass over her. A voice, clipped and controlled:

HUNTER
Junction north — she went north.

More radio traffic. More footsteps, moving away.

She is four meters below the city. She is still.

She waits ninety seconds. The standard sweep interval. Then another sixty, because the interval adjusts after contact.

Silence above the grating.

---

She breathes. Then she takes inventory.

The shoulder: open again. Not badly — the tape held through most of the run and what opened is a surface split, not the deeper work. She reaches into the pack for the field dressings. Does it by feel, in the dark, one-handed, the way she has been doing it since the first week.

Ninety seconds. The wound is dressed. The arm is functional.

She orients. The drainage system connects to the outer freight infrastructure two hundred meters west. The western exit drops her three blocks from the northwest corridor approach — twelve minutes on foot from the staging point where Zara is waiting.

The decoy is running. The camera has her frame. Roman's operators have a confirmed visual at junction node 7, eastern approach, and two hunter teams in pursuit of a woman who is currently four meters underground and moving west.

She moves.


INT. SECURE TRANSIT OFFICE — COMMERCIAL DISTRICT — DAY

A transit company's administrative suite, sublet to a cross-jurisdictional legal liaison office that has not advertised its presence in this building. Third floor. No signage. The window faces an interior courtyard.

Okafor chose it. She has used this room before.

She is already seated when Chen Mei arrives. She does not stand. The room is not large enough for the kind of greeting that would make the meeting feel ordinary, and it is not ordinary. She gestures toward the chair across the table.

Chen Mei sits. She places a polymer sleeve on the table between them. She does not slide it across. It is already positioned correctly — already in reach of the person who needs to open it.

She says nothing. The explanation is inside.

---

Okafor looks at the sleeve for a moment before touching it.

OKAFOR
I need you to be specific about the chain of custody before I open this. Who held it and for how long, in what conditions.

CHEN MEI
Dmitri Koss compiled the documentation over approximately four years. The physical record was extracted by a contact of his who understood its value. From that contact, it passed through two holding stages before reaching me. I have held it for eleven days. Controlled conditions throughout. The format is a polymer-sealed hard copy with a separate digital authentication track on a detached drive, also inside.

She pauses. Then, almost to herself:

CHEN MEI (CONT'D)
Controlled conditions throughout.

OKAFOR
The contact who extracted it.

CHEN MEI
He is no longer alive.

Okafor studies her for a moment. Not assessment — she is already past assessment. Something more careful than that.

OKAFOR
I'm sorry.

CHEN MEI
Thank you.

The room holds this for two seconds. Then Okafor opens the sleeve.
