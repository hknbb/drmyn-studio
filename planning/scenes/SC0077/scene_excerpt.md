```fountain
INT. SERA'S APARTMENT — NIGHT

She has been off the call with Nadia for forty seconds when Morvan calls.

She is still holding her pen.

MORVAN (PHONE)
Sera. I've been trying to reach you for two hours.

SERA
I know. I was — I'm here now.

MORVAN (PHONE)
In terms of our exposure on the ORACLE PRIME material — I sent you the formal review notice this morning. I need to know you received it.

SERA
I received it.

MORVAN (PHONE)
And I need to know that you understand what it means. This isn't an advisory. The legal team has reviewed what you've submitted and they've identified three categories of liability risk that —

SERA
Morvan —

MORVAN (PHONE)
— that require a full evidentiary audit before we can move any further on this. Which means the investigation is paused. Pending review.

She writes. Her pen has not stopped moving since she picked up the call.

SERA
How long is the review.

MORVAN (PHONE)
That depends on what the legal team finds. In terms of our exposure, the timeline is — it's open-ended at this point.

SERA
Open-ended.

MORVAN (PHONE)
Sera, I need you to understand. If this network is what you're suggesting it is, then pursuing it without institutional backing is not an act of journalism, it's — in terms of our exposure it creates a liability profile that the institution cannot absorb.

SERA
I understand what the institution can and cannot absorb, Morvan.

MORVAN (PHONE)
Then you understand why I'm asking you to pause.

A beat. She writes.

SERA
Who asked you to ask me.

A long pause on his end.

MORVAN (PHONE)
That's not — that's a question I'm not going to answer.

SERA
Okay.

MORVAN (PHONE)
In terms of our exposure, I need you to confirm you have paused this. I need to hear you say it.

She keeps writing.

SERA
I've paused it.

MORVAN (PHONE)
Good. I — thank you. I want you to know that if the review clears —

SERA
Right.

MORVAN (PHONE)
— if the review clears, we'll revisit. I'm not killing this permanently. I want you to know that.

A beat. Something shifts in his register — the measured tone drops fractionally. Just once.

MORVAN (PHONE) (CONT'D)
I don't know if this is right.

He says it quietly. Then:

MORVAN (PHONE) (CONT'D)
In terms of our exposure, the review process exists precisely for situations like this. I'll be in touch when legal has completed their —

SERA
I'll be here.

The call ends.

She is still writing.

She writes for another two minutes, filling in everything she remembers from both calls — Nadia's and Morvan's. The notes are organized. Date, time, summary of statements made, exact quotes where she has them. The word "paused" with a timestamp next to it.

She does not pause.

She does not have institutional backing. She does not have legal cover, editorial resources, or a paper of record behind her name. What she has is a documentation chain that she is building at her kitchen table, alone, at night, in an apartment that no one in her institution knows she has been working from for the past three months.

Morvan's "I don't know if this is right" is in the notes. It is timestamped. It is there.

She opens a new file.

At the top she types: INDEPENDENT DOCUMENTATION — ORACLE PRIME — NOT FOR INSTITUTIONAL SUBMISSION.

She keeps writing.
```
