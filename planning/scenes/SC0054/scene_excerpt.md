INT. EASTERN JUNCTION STATION — TRANSIT SHELTER — MIDDAY

The train has discharged its passengers and pulled east. She is in the shelter — a concrete overhang, bench, a departure board showing three connections and two cancellations. She is the only person waiting.

The device signals.

The channel identifier is one she does not recognize. She opens the message.

*I know you're in transit. I have information about your son. I want to speak with you. My terms are simple: tell me what you're planning to do with ORACLE PRIME's broadcast infrastructure. I will tell you everything I know. — Z.O.*

She sits with this for a moment.

Then she opens the channel.

ZARA (V.O.)
My terms stand. Tell me your plan for the broadcast infrastructure and I'll give you everything I have about your situation.

NADIA
You go first.

A pause. Then:

ZARA (V.O.)
All right.

She can hear Zara deciding something. Not hesitating — deciding.

ZARA (V.O.) (CONT'D)
Roman's network has a legal instrument on my daughter. Lena Okonkwo. It was manufactured by the network's legal arm two years ago as a condition of my entry into the hunter pool. It's structured as a debt — principal plus a recalculation mechanism that means each payment increases what I owe. It cannot be paid off. I know this now. I didn't know it when I signed. I've been working toward a floor that isn't there.

NADIA
How much do you owe?

ZARA (V.O.)
The number changes every month. That's the point. The number isn't real. What's real is that they own the obligation and they can call it at any time, which means they own Lena's security at any time.

She is quiet for a moment. Two beats.

ZARA (V.O.) (CONT'D)
I'm telling you this because you need to know why I'm in Roman's network, and because it's the truth, and because I think you'll see the difference between the two. I have four hunters in my field cell. I have operational clearance inside the VELTAIN corridor. I have seen documentation inside Roman's logistics layer.

She pauses here.

ZARA (V.O.) (CONT'D)
Your son. There's a reference in the supply chain routing — facility designation VELTAIN-7, eastern residential block. I don't have a room number or a level. I have the routing reference and a supply log entry from eleven days ago. He was alive and in that facility eleven days ago. That's what I have.

The departure board clicks over. A connection changes times.

Nadia looks at the board for a moment without reading it.

NADIA
Why are you giving me that before I've agreed to anything?

ZARA (V.O.)
Because I said I'd tell you everything I know. That's part of everything I know. My terms are still my terms.

NADIA
What do you actually want from this?

ZARA (V.O.)
I want the machine gone. The debt instrument dies with the network. Lena gets her life back. That's what I want. You're the best chance of that happening that I've seen in two years.

Silence. Not uncomfortable — the kind that happens when something true has been said and both parties know it.

Then:

NADIA
What does Lena think about what you do?

A longer pause.

ZARA (V.O.)
She doesn't —

The sentence does not finish.

Another pause.

ZARA (V.O.) (CONT'D)
She knows I work security. She doesn't know the details. She doesn't —

She stops again.

NADIA
All right.

ZARA (V.O.)
That's not a clean answer.

NADIA
I know.

She is not pressing her. She is not using it. She asked because it was the honest question and now she is letting it be what it is.

ZARA (V.O.)
Your turn. The broadcast infrastructure.

NADIA
I need to reach VELTAIN. I need access to the facility's residential archive. And I need the broadcast relay network to go dark in a coordinated window. That's the plan.

A pause. She can hear Zara processing this.

ZARA (V.O.)
That's either very good or insane.

NADIA
Probably both.

ZARA (V.O.)
I can get you inside the VELTAIN corridor. I have a route that avoids the Transparency Protocol monitoring nodes. I'll need twenty-four hours to set it up.

NADIA
You have the node map?

ZARA (V.O.)
I've been working in this corridor for two years.

NADIA
Send me what you have.

ZARA (V.O.)
I'll send it to this channel when it's ready. Don't move on UNIT 7-ECHO until you hear from me.

NADIA
Why not?

ZARA (V.O.)
Because UNIT 7-ECHO has a passive monitoring node adjacent. If you approach it without the route I'm building, you'll trigger the first Transparency Protocol window early. You'll be visible before the six hours are up.

She considers this.

NADIA
I'll wait. Twenty-four hours.

ZARA (V.O.)
I'll be faster than that.

The line closes.

She sits in the shelter. The departure board shows one more connection, then goes blank on the eastern route.

She has just spoken with a hunter from Roman's network who told her the truth about her daughter and the location of her son.

She does not know yet whether this ends well for either of them.

She opens Marcus's relay documentation and goes back to the node map.
