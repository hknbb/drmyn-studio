INT. TOBIAS'S REPAIR UNIT — REAR ENTRANCE — CONTINUOUS

The door is metal. Set slightly below street grade. No signage.

She knocked forty seconds ago. Two even knocks. Then she waited.

The lock turns from the inside.

TOBIAS ORELL opens the door. He is in his fifties, compact, with the specific kind of stillness that belongs to people who have learned that quick movement draws attention.

He looks at her. He looks at the wrapped forearm. He looks at her again.

He does not open the door wider. He does not close it.

TOBIAS
I don't do this anymore.

She says nothing.

TOBIAS (CONT'D)
Whatever you — I don't. I'm not on any list. I'm not on any network register. That's four years of work and I intend to keep it.

She reaches for the makeshift dressing on her left forearm. Removes it in three efficient movements.

The wound beneath: a draw cut along the radial edge, closed with improvised field material. The wound edges have begun to macerate at the margins — the material is wrong, the compression is wrong — and in approximately seventy minutes this will stop being managed and start compounding.

She holds the arm out.

Not asking. Presenting.

He looks at the wound.

---

Four seconds.

His hands move toward it before he has finished the motion of stepping back.

---

INT. TOBIAS'S REPAIR UNIT — INTERIOR — CONTINUOUS

He has not said she could come in.

She is inside.

At the back, a folding table covered in medical-grade paper — actual medical paper, sized and creased with use. Overhead lamp adjusted for task lighting. Supply cabinet against the wall. He did not set this up just now. This is how the table always is.

He motions at it and moves to the supply cabinet. She sits on the edge of the table and rests the arm. The cabinet opens: instruments in categories, supplies in sequence, nothing misaligned. He selects three items without looking at the labels.

TOBIAS
You understand there's a cost to this.

NADIA
Yes.

TOBIAS
Not the payment kind. The other kind. You were here. I helped you. That becomes a fact. The system that had your location before you walked up to my door now has a data point that puts you in this unit and me adjacent to you. And I have spent four years being adjacent to nothing.

He pauses. He is examining the wound, not looking at her.

TOBIAS (CONT'D)
You knew that when you knocked.

NADIA
Yes.

He sits. He begins.
