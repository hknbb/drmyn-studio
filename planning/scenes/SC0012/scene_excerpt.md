INT. ROMAN'S FORMAL RECEPTION ROOM — VALE RESIDENCE — CONTINUOUS

The document is still on the table. Neither of them has moved far.

ROMAN has returned to his seat, or never fully left it. NADIA stands near the window, her back to the room for a moment — not dramatically; she turned to collect herself, and now she turns back.

She sits. She folds her hands on the table.

---

NADIA
I want to propose a resolution.

ROMAN
Go ahead.

NADIA
Full compliance. Whatever terms you need — financial, legal, operational. Prenuptial provisions, additional disclosures. I will sign whatever you prepare.

ROMAN
I see.

NADIA
I'll leave the residence. I won't contest anything. No claim on the property, no claim on the name. I'll take what I came with and nothing else.

ROMAN
That's a significant offer.

He means this. He is not being ironic.

NADIA
There's more.

ROMAN
I assumed there would be.

NADIA
I won't speak about any of it. The affair — Marcus — the nature of the pregnancy. Anyone who asks will hear that the marriage ended by mutual agreement. I'll sign a non-disclosure on whatever scope you specify. Including the paternity.

A beat.

ROMAN
That covers everyone.

NADIA
Everyone. Permanently.

He looks at the document between them. He appears to be reviewing the terms she has laid out — the way you review something in order to respond to it with appropriate consideration.

ROMAN
May I ask something.

NADIA
Yes.

ROMAN
Do you believe you would honor those terms? If I agreed to them?

A beat. This is not the question she expected.

NADIA
Yes.

ROMAN
I believe you.

He says this simply. He means it. There is something in his voice that is almost respect — or would be, in a different context.

A pause.

ROMAN (CONT'D)
It doesn't change the terms.

She looks at him.

NADIA
You haven't told me what the terms are.

ROMAN
No.

NADIA
Then I'm offering without a counter-position. I'm offering everything I have without knowing what you want in return.

ROMAN
I know.

Silence.

The room does not shift. The light does not change. The document sits where it has been sitting. Roman's expression is the same expression it has been throughout the conversation.

Nadia looks at him and understands something she did not understand when she sat down.

She looks away.

NADIA
(quietly)
You were never going to negotiate.

ROMAN
No.

Not cruel. Not triumphant. Administrative.

NADIA
This was —

She does not finish the sentence.

ROMAN
I wanted to know what you had. Now I know.

He stands. Straightens his jacket. The same gesture as before.

ROMAN (CONT'D)
You'll be contacted about the formal process.

He leaves.
