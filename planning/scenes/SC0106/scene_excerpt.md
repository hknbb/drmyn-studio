INT. VELTAIN COMPOUND — BROADCAST TERMINAL ROOM — NIGHT

A functional room on Roman's private level: two broadcast infrastructure terminals against the far wall, a monitoring display above them showing the ORACLE PRIME platform's current status — The Herald's feed restored and running, the betting line active, the system presenting as intact. Between the door and the terminals, a distance of approximately six meters.

ROMAN is at the monitoring display when Nadia enters.

He turns. He takes her in — the closure tape on her left side, the state of her hands, the absence of any visible weapon, the specific quality of her posture that is not the posture he married. He takes all of this in, and the taking-in is visible only as a brief pause before he speaks. Roman does not show surprise. He shows adjustment.

ROMAN
(measured; the full weight of eleven days finding its register)
You've survived longer than I projected. That's not a criticism. It's an acknowledgment that my model required revision.

Nadia moves into the room. Not toward him. Toward the terminals.

ROMAN (CONT'D)
The game's infrastructure is designed to reach a natural terminus. What you're experiencing as an ending of your making — it isn't. The system closes events. It has closed thirteen before this one. The terminus is built in.

She is reading the terminals as she moves. The broadcast infrastructure terminal is the left-hand unit — she knows this from Marcus's package; he had the schematics; she memorized them. The card reader is at the terminal's base.

She is four meters away.

ROMAN (CONT'D)
(reframing; his voice does not change register; this is still a conversation he believes he is setting the terms of)
I want to tell you something about what happens next, in the interest of accurate information. The game's outcome does not require your death. It requires the game's conclusion. Those are separable terms. I've separated them before.

Three meters.

ROMAN (CONT'D)
Jin is in the building.

She stops. One step — her stride has a natural period and she stops in the middle of it, foot not yet down, and she places it carefully and is still.

ROMAN (CONT'D)
(reading her with the precision he uses for all assets)
He's well. He's been here since the fourth day. I relocated him from the external facility because the facility's security became a liability. He is two floors below us. He is not — he has not been harmed.

A beat.

NADIA
Where.

ROMAN
That's the offer. You stop what you came here to do — and I know what you came here to do; you've been in the room — and I tell you where he is, and the game closes, and you both leave. That's the actual terminus. The one that's available tonight.

She turns toward the terminal.

ROMAN (CONT'D)
Nadia.

The first time he has said her name in this scene. His voice on it does not change — still measured — but the name is a different register than asset pricing and he knows it.

She inserts the card.

BROADCAST INFRASTRUCTURE TERMINAL — STATUS DISPLAY:

[ADMINISTRATOR CARD ACCEPTED — INITIATING SYSTEM TERMINATION PROTOCOL]

The terminal does not alarm. It acknowledges. A quiet confirmation tone — the sound of a system accepting a valid command — and then the status display on the monitoring screen above shifts: the ORACLE PRIME platform feed flickers; the betting line freezes; The Herald's voice cuts to silence mid-sentence for the second time tonight, but this time the silence is different; this time the system is not being interrupted; it is being ended.

Roman watches this.

His face: the specific expression of a man who has priced everything incorrectly and knows in this moment exactly which variable he failed to account for — not her capability; not her intelligence; not her physical endurance. Her refusal to be priced at all. He built a system that converts people into assets and prices. She never became either.

He moves.

Not toward her — toward the door. He is going to the technical control room. He knows the shutdown sequence has a terminal stage that requires physical insertion at the server core; the card has begun the process but has not completed it. There is still a window. This is Roman: he is not fleeing; he is redirecting to the correct operational response.

He passes her on the way to the door. Two meters between them. Neither of them moves toward the other.

At the door:

ROMAN
(without turning back; precise; something that is neither threat nor concession but the last honest accounting between them)
You won't be able to find him without me.

He leaves.

Nadia is alone with the terminal. The monitoring display shows the shutdown sequence's progress — a percentage indicator that is climbing — and the ORACLE PRIME platform fragmenting in real time as the backend begins its collapse.

She pulls the card from the terminal. She needs it for the server core.

She looks at the monitoring display for one second. The Herald's silence is total. The betting line is gone. Fourteen events' worth of infrastructure beginning to close.

She turns toward the door Roman used.

She is going to find Jin.
