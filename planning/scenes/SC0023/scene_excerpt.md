She returns to the filed observation from the warehouse passage.

SC0016: she heard it indirectly during the game's opening broadcast — a note she had not yet applied. 'ORACLE PRIME operates on a commitment to data integrity. The odds you see on your interface reflect real-time aggregate wagering. We do not adjust, estimate, or project. What the system reports is what exists.'

She has one reliable information source inside the system.

She cannot use it yet. She has no mechanism for feeding the system false inputs, no way to test whether the odds will track misinformation she introduces. But the rule is identified. It is the first asymmetry she has found.
