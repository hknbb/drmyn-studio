INT. DRAINAGE CONDUIT — CONTINUOUS

Dark. The sound of their movement echoes against concrete on three sides. Nadia is ahead. Zara is behind her — breathing controlled, audible only because the conduit amplifies everything.

Nadia stops. Listens.

From the direction they came — boots on the embankment's crown. A flashlight beam crosses the drainage cover's edge. Stops. Moves on.

They wait.

Thirty seconds.

The boots move north along the embankment toward the junction. The frequency pings continue, moving away.

ZARA
(low, in the dark)
How long.

NADIA
Until the burst cycle ends. Four minutes. After that the eastern element collapses back and the corridor fills.

ZARA
And the cut runs to the northwest approach.

NADIA
Yes.

A silence. In it, Zara is pressing something against the wound — a compress from her field kit, or her hand, or both. She does this without asking for help and without performing that she doesn't need it.

NADIA (CONT'D)
(low)
It went through.

ZARA
I know.

NADIA
That's —

ZARA
I know what it is.

A pause.

NADIA
We have the kit from the cache.

ZARA
After the cut.

She is not denying the wound. She is deferring the treatment to the correct interval — which is what Nadia would have said if their positions were reversed. The parallel transaction logic, still running. They are still, in the conduit's dark, two professionals respecting each other's capacity to manage their own damage.

NADIA
After the cut.

A beat.

ZARA
(very quiet)
You came back before I was down.

NADIA
You'd have done the same.

ZARA
I don't know that yet.

She says it without deflection, without false modesty. It is a statement about the current state of the evidence: she doesn't know yet whether she would have. The honesty is the same register she uses for everything. Nadia hears it.

She does not answer.

The burst cycle runs out at the four-minute mark. South of them — faint frequency ping, then silence. The eastern element has collapsed back toward the main corridor; the broadcast misdirection's window has closed.

They move.
