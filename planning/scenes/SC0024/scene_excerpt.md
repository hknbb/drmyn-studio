EXT. INDUSTRIAL LAYER — SERVICE ROAD JUNCTION — MORNING

The HALO UNIT is already there when she reaches the junction.

Not following her. Already positioned. The difference matters: following means reactive deployment, already positioned means logistical anticipation — it knew where she was going before she finished going there. She catalogs this and does not slow her pace.

She stops when she is two meters from it. Same distance as the warehouse passage. She has learned the geometry of these conversations.

THE HERALD (V.O.)
Good morning. We noticed you've been busy.

NADIA
What are the odds.

THE HERALD (V.O.)
SURVIVAL, forty-eight hours: 2.9. Up from this morning's low. Our audience has been revising their models based on the contact sequence. You've outperformed the analytical projection by a meaningful margin. Some of our subscribers find no-baseline subjects outperform consistently — they've been making the case in the commentary feeds and the market is moving with them.

The numbers arrive with that fractional clipped precision — shorter clauses, no flourish. She notes it. It is the same tell as the warehouse passage. The structural constraint. The numbers are real.

Everything else is managed.

THE HERALD (V.O.) (CONT'D)
What I wanted to discuss — if you have a moment —

NADIA
I have a moment.

THE HERALD (V.O.)
Your survey of this district this morning was deliberate. Pattern-reading, not aimless movement. Our broadcast captured you working. I want to be transparent: whatever you were looking for, we saw you looking. I'm not telling you this to discourage the behavior — I'm telling you because the broadcast audience found it compelling, and compelling behavior moves markets.

She processes this. The temporal gap question — deferred in the SC0023 model — has just been reopened. Either the sweep captured her reconnaissance despite the longer interval, or The Herald is inferring and presenting as observation. She cannot determine which from this statement.

She does not reveal the uncertainty.

NADIA
What else.

THE HERALD (V.O.)
Now, here's what's interesting —

She registers the tell. Whatever follows is weaponized.

THE HERALD (V.O.) (CONT'D)
— you've been operating with genuine tactical sophistication. Your military record is part of our subscriber documentation — the audience knows your training history. But there's a gap between knowing a subject has training and watching her demonstrate it under pressure. You've demonstrated it. The market has responded accordingly.

THE HERALD (V.O.) (CONT'D)

Yes. And right now, what the event needs is for you to be able to function. A subject who is distracted by uncertainty about irrelevant variables performs less well. Worse event. So I'm going to give you a relevant update and ask nothing in return.

She does not respond. She lets him deliver it.

THE HERALD (V.O.) (CONT'D)
Jin is well. He is being kept in a climate-controlled environment, fed on schedule. He is wearing the blue bracelet — the hospital bracelet from his birth registration. One beat.

The image The Herald just put in front of her is not a threat. It is specific. A patch of light on the floor. He reached for it and laughed. This is what he does — she knows this; he has done it at home, in the morning light through the kitchen window, he has done it and she has watched him do it. The image is real because the detail is real.

The image is also the system demonstrating that it was in the room with him this morning and she was not.

THE HERALD (V.O.) (CONT'D)
He doesn't know what's happening. That's the age. He's warm, he's fed, he's interested in the world. Whatever Roman's arrangements are on that side, they include adequate care. I'm telling you this because a mother who is operating on incomplete information about her child is a different kind of problem than a mother who has been given a confirmed update. You now have a confirmed update.

NADIA

THE HERALD (V.O.)
This morning. Within the last two hours.

One more item — genuinely informational, not framing. The field has expanded. Two additional contracted positions entered deployment in the last forty minutes.
