INT. ROMAN'S PRIVATE OFFICE — VELTAIN COMPOUND — DAY

The office is at the compound's upper level. The window faces west. Afternoon light, direct. Roman is standing when Dimitri arrives. He does not sit. He does not offer a seat.

Dimitri closes the door behind him. He does not check his watch.

ROMAN
The Okafor filing.

DIMITRI
Cross-jurisdictional financial crimes. She initiated last night.

ROMAN
I know what she initiated. I'm telling you what I know. The filing was against the registered financial instruments — not the operational layer. She is building from the outside in.

DIMITRI
Cass has already challenged the first jurisdiction's standing. The challenge is sound.

ROMAN
The challenge is sound and it will fail.

He turns slightly toward the window. Not away from Dimitri — a partial angle, observational.

ROMAN (CONT'D)
She filed in two jurisdictions simultaneously. The Nordic registry confirmation landed at ten twenty-three. Cass did not anticipate the parallel filing. That tells me the parallel filing was not in our legal preparation document.

DIMITRI
It wasn't. The Nordic instrument was registered under a subsidiary threshold — it didn't appear in the primary infrastructure profile.

ROMAN
Understand — that is not a gap in Cass's preparation. That is a gap in our profile. Someone gave Okafor documentation that is not in our own systems.

He turns the pen in his hands. Once. Then sets it down.

ROMAN (CONT'D)
The documentation chain has one origin. Dimitri's operational logs. The full financial architecture at the subsidiary threshold level exists in one place.

A silence. He does not look at Dimitri when he says the name. He has already moved past the accusation, past the conversation that would follow. He states the analysis the way he states all analysis: as the data, not the implication.

DIMITRI
The material moved through Marcus Chen's intelligence network. His sister is the carrier.

ROMAN
Chen Mei. She arrived at the terminal six days ago. She is now under Okafor's institutional protection — she moved before I could identify the channel.

DIMITRI
She is with Okafor's team. A protected location inside their jurisdictional coverage.

ROMAN
Then access is complicated.

DIMITRI
It can be managed.

ROMAN
It cannot be managed in a way that does not generate additional documentation inside Okafor's coverage jurisdiction. Which means it cannot be managed quietly.

He is still. The stillness is working now — the quality of it has changed, very slightly, from ease to maintenance. Anyone in the room with the correct knowledge of Roman's behavioral register would clock it. Dimitri clocks it.

DIMITRI
I'll handle it.

ROMAN
When.

DIMITRI
In the window before the second jurisdiction begins producing formal requests. Two days, perhaps three.

Roman looks at him.

He looks at him for a moment longer than the sentence requires. The nod, when it comes, is small.

ROMAN
Good.
