EXT. NORTHERN TRANSIT CORRIDOR — OUTER DISTRICT PERIMETER — NIGHT

The corridor runs between a sealed cargo processing facility on the east side and a long concrete drainage embankment on the west. Overhead lighting at two-hundred-meter intervals. Active. Each light a pool and then darkness and then another pool.

Nadia is moving through the dark between the second and third lights. Zara is eight meters behind her, same pace, same interval from the wall.

They have been moving this way for eleven minutes.

Nadia stops at the edge of the third light's pool. Holds up one hand without looking back.

She counts the lights ahead. Four more before the junction that marks the corridor's end. One hundred sixty meters, approximately. The embankment runs continuous. No cover on the western side until the drainage cut at the junction.

She looks back at Zara.

NADIA
(low)
Two teams. Not one.

ZARA
How do you know?

NADIA
The spacing on the comms ping. One source, two different delay intervals. Eastern approach and here.

A beat.

ZARA
The broadcast signal.

NADIA
It's still running.

Zara processes this. The passive burst cycle — twelve minutes, eastern approach junction node 7, a false position signal still feeding into the ORACLE PRIME grid on the interval they set it. Nadia placed herself there three hours ago. The grid accepted it.

ZARA
One element went east.

NADIA
And one came here.

ZARA
How many here.

NADIA
At least eight. Moving from the south junction. They have three minutes on us, maybe less.

She looks at the drainage embankment. Runs the geometry. The gap in the deployment documents — the coverage gap Zara's finger traced in the cache site, the gap Nadia had already moved to before Zara finished tracing it — runs along the northwest quadrant of the perimeter. Fifty meters past the junction. If the eastern element doesn't collapse back in time.

NADIA (CONT'D)
The coverage gap holds for six minutes after the junction. If we're past the drainage cut when the burst cycle ends.

ZARA
And if we're not.

NADIA
We are.

She moves into the third light pool. No sprint — controlled pace, economy of motion, the kind of movement that reads as normal to a wide-angle lens and doesn't produce a heat signature spike in ORACLE PRIME's infrared grid.

Zara follows.

They pass under the third light. Into darkness. Under the fourth.

Somewhere south of them — a frequency ping. Brief. Military economy. DIMITRI's voice on the command channel, stripped to minimum.

DIMITRI (V.O.)
(on radio, distant)
Northern corridor, confirm contact.

A response, unintelligible at distance. Another ping.

DIMITRI (V.O.)
Accelerate. He was ahead of us once.

The 'he' — wrong pronoun, wrong person. He is working from a profile that has not been updated for what she has become. Roman would not have said 'he.' Roman reads her file; Roman knows who he's hunting. Dimitri is hunting the file's summary, not the person.

Nadia notes this. It does not slow her.

They reach the fifth light. One hundred meters to the junction. The concrete drainage embankment curves west here, following the old canal bed. The wall's surface changes — newer pour, slightly lighter, where a section was repaired.

Nadia stops.

She presses two fingers against the repair seam. Listens.

NADIA
(very low)
There's a team on the other side.

ZARA
How —

NADIA
The seam. It's absorbing vibration it shouldn't. Someone's weight on the other side.

A moment. Zara looks north at the junction. One hundred meters. The drainage cut is visible as a shadow against the embankment's base.

ZARA
How many.

NADIA
One. Maybe two.

ZARA
Advance element. They came around.

Which means the main force is behind them. Which means the geometry has changed. They are not moving toward a gap. They are moving into a closing.

Nadia looks at the drainage cut. She looks at the timing. She looks at the embankment's repair seam and the shadow of the cut and the distance.

NADIA
We go through, not around.

ZARA
There are —

NADIA
One. Maybe two. The main force is three minutes south. The cut is a hundred meters. We go through.

She is not asking.

Zara reads her. A moment in which neither of them speaks and the calculation is made without words. The transactional logic — the parallel terms, the stated conditions, the ledger of what each woman owes the other — is suspended for exactly as long as the math requires. Then it isn't suspended anymore. It's just what they're doing.

ZARA
Through the seam or over?

NADIA
Over.

They move.

The embankment is three meters high. A drainage pipe bracket at two meters provides the step. Nadia reaches the top in four seconds. Stays flat against the surface. Looks over.

An advance operative — one — posted at the embankment's far base, facing north toward the junction. Not looking back. He does not expect approach from the wall he is leaning against.

Nadia drops.

The operative goes down quickly. Not dead — she needs the corridor behind her quiet, not sounding an alarm. He is quiet.

She signals up to Zara.

Zara comes over the top. She lands correctly — weight distributed, controlled — and then something is wrong.

She goes down on one knee.

Nadia is at her side in the four seconds before the calculation catches up to the movement.

NADIA
(very low)
Where.

ZARA
Left side. High. Something from the north junction — I didn't —

She stops. Breathes. Her hand is at her side below the jacket. When she pulls it back the dark is darker.

ZARA (CONT'D)
I didn't hear it.

A suppressed round. From the north junction — an element they hadn't placed, a third vector, the part of Dimitri's deployment that exceeded what any standard-parameter hunt would have fielded. Roman deploys precision. Dimitri deployed area.

Nadia looks at her hand. Looks at the direction of the north junction. Looks at the fifty meters between them and the drainage cut.

She does not look at the wound to assess it. She has already assessed it by feel, by the location of Zara's hand, by the quality of the breathing. She knows what it is.

NADIA
(still very low)
Can you move.

ZARA
Yes.

She says it the way she states everything — flat, declarative, no performance in it. Her voice does not ask Nadia to believe her. It simply reports.

NADIA
Then we move now.

She gets her shoulder under Zara's arm without asking. Zara does not resist this and does not lean into it more than the geometry requires. They are moving before the position is fully settled.

Fifty meters.

The drainage cut opens at the base of the embankment — a meter-wide conduit, three decades of concrete grime, running northwest into the outer perimeter's maintenance infrastructure. Low. They will have to bend. Zara will have to bend.

They reach it.

Behind them, south of the embankment — comms frequency activity. Multiple pings. The main element has found the advance operative.

DIMITRI (V.O.)
(on radio, closer now)
Northern embankment. All elements converge.

Nadia pulls the drainage cover. It is heavy and it is old and it moves.

They go in.
