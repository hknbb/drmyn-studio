INT. VALE ENTERPRISE BUILDING — ROMAN'S OPERATIONAL FLOOR — VARDOVA — DAY

The floor is designed not to look like a command center. It looks like a workspace — high ceilings, muted surfaces, expensive restraint. The screens are there if you know to find them.

ROMAN VALE stands at the far end of the room with the stillness of a man who has never been in a hurry. He is looking at nothing in particular. Looking at everything.

One wall carries a low-resolution feed — a city at night, compressed and slightly grainy. Crowds moving. Numbers scrolling at the margin. It does not demand attention. It is simply on.

DIMITRI KOSS enters from a side corridor. No knock. No announcement. He knows the protocols.

DIMITRI
Merin closed out.

ROMAN
When.

DIMITRI
Forty minutes ago. Clean.

Roman turns slightly. He doesn't look at the feed.

ROMAN
Yield.

DIMITRI
Below projection. The field ran short.

A beat. Roman is not disappointed. He is recalibrating.

ROMAN
Understand — short fields compress the window. The audience doesn't have time to commit. We lose the late volume.

DIMITRI
Jann flagged it. He's adjusting the intake parameters for the next run.

ROMAN
Tell him to adjust the floor, not the ceiling. The ceiling is where the money is.

Dimitri marks something on a small device. No further comment.

Roman moves toward a low cabinet along the inner wall. He opens it — not to retrieve anything. A habit, or a ritual. Inside: a collection of objects, mundane in themselves. A field badge. A wristwatch. A cracked lens from a pair of glasses.

ROMAN (CONT'D)
The Merin yield goes into reserve. Don't let Cass reallocate it.

DIMITRI
Understood.

ROMAN
And have her clean the intake contracts before the next run. The northern lane has been cited twice. Once is a fee. Twice is a pattern.

He closes the cabinet.

DIMITRI
Do you want visibility on the next field before it opens?

Roman considers this the way a man considers whether to order dessert.

ROMAN
No.

He moves back toward the screens. The city feed is still running. The numbers at the margin have changed. He glances at them briefly — not with interest, with inventory.

ROMAN (CONT'D)
What's the current position on the Vardova book.

DIMITRI
Flat. Nothing unusual.

ROMAN
Keep it flat.

Dimitri goes. Roman stays where he is, looking at the screens.

The feed continues. The numbers continue. Roman's expression does not change.

Outside the windows: VARDOVA at mid-morning. A city that doesn't know this room exists.
