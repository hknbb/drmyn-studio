EXT. RENNMARK DISTRICT ALLEY — CONTINUOUS

Twenty meters from the rear entrance.

She is moving north, in the cut between the buildings, at the pace of someone who belongs in the alley. Her left arm is at her side. The dressing is correct. The surveillance gap Tobias identified is still open — she has approximately forty seconds of it remaining.

The HALO UNIT at the far end of the cut activates.

THE HERALD (V.O.)
(broadcast register, warm)
Now, here's what's interesting — the event's field has expanded. Our contestant has made her first confirmed associate contact: a medical service provider in the Rennmark district. Our network's standard protocol recognizes associate events and extends the field accordingly. The bounty adjustment is effective now. New odds on the associate apprehension are live for our audience. This isn't a penalty — it's an expansion. Nadia has found a helper. The game is getting more interesting.

The HALO UNIT's sensor array is not oriented toward her. It is delivering a general broadcast to the district. It does not need to find her to harm her.

She stops.

She looks at the HALO UNIT.

She turns back.

---

INT. TOBIAS'S REPAIR UNIT — REAR ENTRANCE

She reaches the door. She raises her hand to knock.

The door opens before she knocks.

TOBIAS stands in the doorway. He is holding the edge of the door. He does not step back. He is at exactly door-width — not opening, not closing.

He heard it too.

They look at each other.

Both already know. There is a version of this moment in which something needs to be explained, and this is not that version.

TOBIAS
You need to go.

NADIA
I came back to —

TOBIAS
I know why you came back. You need to go anyway.

He is not angry. He is not panicked. He is — as he has been across all three of these scenes — procedurally calm. He is managing the situation the way he manages a wound: categorize, address, move.

TOBIAS (CONT'D)
I was invisible for four years. That's — that's gone now. That's a fact. You leaving in the next thirty seconds doesn't change the fact. It slightly reduces the exposure window. That's what you can do.

NADIA
I'm sorry.

A beat. He looks at her. Whatever the apology means to him, he receives it without softening and without dismissing it.

TOBIAS
Go.

She goes.

---

EXT. RENNMARK DISTRICT ALLEY — CONTINUOUS

She walks north.

Behind her, the HALO UNIT continues its broadcast rotation. The Herald does not need her presence to keep reporting. The odds are live. The field has expanded. The audience is engaged.

She files Tobias's name in the same column as the cost — as a fact that cannot be undone, only carried.

She does not look back.
