INT. ROMAN'S PRIVATE OFFICE — VALE RESIDENCE — NIGHT

Not a study. An office. The distinction matters: there are no personal objects here that were not chosen to be seen. Two chairs, a desk, a glass of water that has not been touched. The room operates at a specific temperature.

ROMAN stands near the desk — not behind it. He does not use the desk as a barrier when it matters that distance not feel adversarial.

DIMITRI sits. He has a leather-bound ledger on the table beside him. He is always in possession of a ledger. This is not discussed.

ROMAN
The Corsetti audit came back clean.

DIMITRI
I expected it to.

ROMAN
So did I.

A pause. Roman moves to the window. Looks out, or appears to — his eyes have the quality of a man whose attention is elsewhere.

ROMAN (CONT'D)
There's a potential problem. Intelligence category. Early stage, unconfirmed.

DIMITRI
Internal or external.

ROMAN
Internal.

DIMITRI
How contained.

ROMAN
One source. Possibly. I'm still pricing it.

Dimitri's expression does not change. This is the correct response and both men know it.

ROMAN (CONT'D)
I want a secondary verification run on a personnel subset.

He takes a folded paper from the desk and crosses the room to hand it to Dimitri. Not a dramatic gesture — the transfer of a document. Dimitri takes it. Opens it.

He reads the list. His face gives nothing. He takes the ledger, opens it to a clean page, and begins making notes.

The pen moves. Roman watches it.

DIMITRI
Timeline.

ROMAN
Seventy-two hours for preliminary. I'll tell you if it changes.

DIMITRI
Do you want me to run it personally or —

ROMAN
Personally.

Dimitri nods. He continues noting. He does not ask what the intelligence problem concerns. He does not ask who Roman suspects. The questions are obvious. His not asking them is the most precise thing he does in the room.

Roman watches him not ask.

ROMAN (CONT'D)
That's everything.

DIMITRI
I'll have something for you by Thursday.

He closes the ledger. Stands. The paper is in his jacket pocket now; he transferred it without Dimitri appearing to have done so.

ROMAN
Dimitri.

Dimitri pauses at the door.

ROMAN (CONT'D)
This stays in this room.

DIMITRI
It always does.

He leaves.
