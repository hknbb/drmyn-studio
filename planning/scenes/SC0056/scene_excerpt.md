INT. VELTAIN — ROMAN'S OPERATIONS CENTER — NIGHT

A long room. Clean surfaces. A display wall running behavioral analytics from the ORACLE PRIME feed. No debris, no personality in the objects. A room that belongs to a man who does not need the room to tell him who he is.

Roman stands at the display. Not reading — he has read it. He is looking at it in the way that follows reading.

The display shows two overlapping movement pattern maps:

EVENT MOVEMENT PROFILE — SC: NADIA VALE
PREVIOUS 96-HOUR WINDOW: Coverage area 4.2km² | Displacement pattern: non-directional | Concealment index: 87% | Risk-exposure tolerance: LOW
CURRENT 18-HOUR WINDOW: Coverage area 31.6km² | Displacement pattern: directional (bearing: NNW) | Concealment index: 44% | Risk-exposure tolerance: ELEVATED
BEHAVIORAL CONFIDENCE: 96% | DESTINATION: UNRESOLVED | TRANSIT CORRIDOR DEGRADATION: ACTIVE

Dimitri is at the far end of the room. He has the same data on a tablet. He brings the summary when Roman does not turn.

DIMITRI
Subject behavioral change confirmed. Pattern shift at
ninety-six percent confidence. Movement is directional —
northnorthwest bearing, consistent across three corridor
transitions. She is not evading. She is going somewhere.

He waits.

DIMITRI (CONT'D)
Standard pressure protocol would be logical. Redeploy the
two-hunter sweep, compress the corridor window.

A pause.

DIMITRI (CONT'D)
The subject's behavioral profile suggests she has acquired
operational intelligence she should not have. The event has
run fourteen days. The clean option is a managed resolution
with a standard payout structure. I can have a proposal
prepared in forty minutes.

He is advising Roman to end it.

Roman does not answer.

He looks at the behavioral data again — specifically at the eighteen-hour window, the 44% concealment index. She stopped hiding. She is going somewhere and she is going there with a plan and she committed to it while he was not watching.

He does not say this.

ROMAN
Understand — what she's changed is the timeline. Not the
outcome.

He turns from the display.

ROMAN (CONT'D)
Activate the corridor rescue protocol. The pre-positioned
safe route in the NNW corridor. Genuine resources, genuine
geography. Everything she needs for the next operational phase.

DIMITRI
The Linden Junction package.

ROMAN
Position it in her projected path within the next six hours.
She will move toward it because it is genuinely useful. She
has good intelligence — she will check it. Let it hold.

Dimitri makes a note.

DIMITRI
Confirmed.

Nothing more is said about ending the event.

Roman turns back to the display. The behavioral data is still there — the two windows, the shift, the unresolved destination. He looks at the directional vector: NNW, consistent across three transitions.

She is going to VELTAIN.

He has not said this out loud. He does not say it now. He prices it in and moves on.

Dimitri is already working. He does not check his watch.
