Chen Mei straightens the corner of the polymer sleeve. It does not need straightening. She straightens it.

Okafor reads the third section.

---

Dimitri's documentation of Roman's override decisions. Every instance in which Roman overrode Dimitri's assessment with a financial argument. Every instance in which Dimitri recorded the override, framed as a procedural note. The framing is careful: it is written as risk management, which means it is also a ledger of his own compliance, documented in his own hand, preserved because he wanted a record of not being the one who decided.

He wanted to be able to say: I noted the risk. He did not understand that noting the risk, while continuing, while recording that you continued, is also a record.

Okafor closes the folder.

OKAFOR

CHEN MEI
Sera Mast.

OKAFOR
You know her.

CHEN MEI
She was contacted through a protected channel. I don't know what she currently holds. I know the channel was established.

That was the intention. That was always the intention.
