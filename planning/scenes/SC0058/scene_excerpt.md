INT. REGIONAL BUS — FORWARD SECTION — LATE AFTERNOON

Ninety minutes from LINDEN JUNCTION. The light through the window is going flat and grey; the corridor outside is industrial now, relay infrastructure along the road margins.

The device alerts.

New message. Third channel — the corridor-operative relay node she has been monitoring since the first week. A broadcast channel used by people in transit between protected regions. She has never sent through it. She has only listened. This channel is also monitored by Roman's network. She has always known that.

The message is addressed to her by the code name she used in the first seven days of the event, before she stopped using it.

She opens it.

RESCUE ROUTE — LINDEN JUNCTION
Safe location: 14 HAVEL COURT, northern service quarter, third floor, east-facing unit.
Contact phrase: the light's been out since Tuesday.
Supplies confirmed on-site: medical (wound management, antibiotic, suture), clothing (active, size noted), nutrition (72-hour supply), communication hardware (encrypted relay, pre-keyed).
Exit route: northnorthwest from LINDEN JUNCTION — VELTAIN corridor via the SORAL access road, clear to the facility perimeter.
Window: forty-eight hours.

She reads it once. Then she opens the ORACLE PRIME betting feed on a secondary partition of the device.

She has been reading the feed since SC0053 — since The Herald announced the Transparency Protocol and she understood that the odds move when the audience can see her. The Herald cannot fabricate live betting data. It is the one thing the system cannot adjust: when a development is genuine and unexpected, the audience bets in volume and contradiction — a chaotic spike, high variance, before the market finds a new consensus.

The odds are moving.

They are not moving chaotically.

They are moving in a single direction — a smooth, coordinated, large-amplitude shift upward on SUBJECT REACHES VELTAIN — arriving at a new consensus in under forty seconds. Clean. Confident. Pre-priced.

The market was already priced in before she read the message.

She opens a new document. She writes:

*Marker one: timing.*

The route was positioned in her projected corridor, viable within four hours of the behavioral shift Roman read from The Herald's feed. She shifted behavior on the eastbound train. A response was operational at LINDEN JUNCTION, approximately ninety minutes from her current position, by the time she was three hours past eastern junction. Genuine underground rescue — assembled with this supply calibration, this exit route specificity, this resource completeness — takes days. Four hours means whoever built this had her movement profile almost simultaneously with her making it. The only entity with simultaneous access to that profile is Roman's network.

*Marker two: calibration.*

Wound management, not initial treatment. Clothing in her size and activity rating — not a general size, not a supply-chain default. Food in the correct portion volume for someone at her metabolic state after fourteen days of sustained physical stress. This manifest was written for Nadia Vale in the past forty-eight hours. Not for a subject in general. For her, specifically, in her current condition. A genuine rescue assembled at speed uses what is available. This used what was selected.

*Marker three: the odds.*

The ORACLE PRIME audience bets on information. When the rescue route appeared on this channel — a channel Roman's network monitors — and was matched to her code name, the audience's betting behavior should have reflected genuine uncertainty about whether she would accept or recognize the route as a trap. It did not. The audience moved to consensus in forty seconds. The variance was negligible. They were already positioned. The market does not pre-price an organic development. It pre-prices an announced one.

She looks at the three items.

One beat.

Then:

*Decision: proceed toward LINDEN JUNCTION. Use what can be confirmed genuine. Watch for the activation mechanism. Do not stay beyond what acquisition requires.*

The route is controlled. The supplies inside it are real. The exit route from it is real. The trap has been built using genuine materials because a trap built from fake materials is one she would leave immediately. Roman built her a real rescue and put a door at the back of it.

She needs the medical supplies. She needs the communication hardware. She needs the exit route northnorthwest.

She cannot acquire these from any other source before the VELTAIN perimeter. She has checked. There is no alternative chain.

She puts the device away.

Outside: the industrial corridor continues. A relay tower. Another. The bus does not slow.

She is going in.
