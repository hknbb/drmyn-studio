INT. MERIN — QUAY DISTRICT, UNIT 14-B — MORNING

The unit is a converted freight bay: low concrete ceiling, one high window facing the quay, light that smells like salt and diesel. Crates against the west wall. A folding table. A camp stove that has not been used today.

OTTO is already there when NADIA arrives. He is sixty, or seventy, or the particular age that people who have spent their lives in operational work look when you meet them for the first time — which is: all of it, at once. He does not offer his hand.

OTTO
He said you'd come.

He produces the phone from a locked case beneath the table. A handset — industrial-grade, no manufacturer markings. He sets it on the table between them.

OTTO (CONT'D)
He said you'd know the code.

She does.

He moves to the far end of the room. He turns his back. He looks out the high window at the grey water. This is the only thing he has left to give her, and he gives it without being asked.

NADIA enters the access code: 7-3-1-1-4. The phone activates. A single audio file. No label.

She presses play.

A beat of ambient noise — wherever he recorded it, there was wind. Then:

MARCUS (V.O.)
If you're hearing this, then things went the way I was afraid they'd go. I'm sorry. I wanted more time.

She does not stop the message.

MARCUS (V.O.) (CONT'D)
The phone has an encrypted partition — secondary code is the date we met, reversed. In the partition: everything I had on Roman. Network structure, financial infrastructure, the broadcast system, the names. It's complete. I spent two years on it.

She knew something was wrong in the last weeks before he died. She read it as guilt — the particular attentiveness of a man preparing to confess. It was not that. It was this.

MARCUS (V.O.) (CONT'D)
I kept it from you. I told myself it was safer for you not to know. That was wrong. I know that now. I thought the distance would protect you. It didn't protect either of us.

She keeps listening.

MARCUS (V.O.) (CONT'D)
Roman has done this before. There's documentation in the package. All of them. You need to know that.

She does not know yet what that means. She will know in the next hour.

MARCUS (V.O.) (CONT'D)
In the package — look for the broadcast architecture file. There's a maintenance access layer in the ORACLE PRIME infrastructure. That's what stops it. Not Roman. The broadcast. Find that file.

She is listening to a dead man tell her how to fight. She is listening because it is the only way left to be with him.

MARCUS (V.O.) (CONT'D)
Karev.

One word. Her maiden name. His private anchor for her.

She holds it.

MARCUS (V.O.) (CONT'D)
Take Jin home.

The message ends.

The ambient noise cuts. Silence.

OTTO has not moved from the window. He stays there.

NADIA looks at the phone in her hand. She does not put it down.

She does the thing she has always done when she needs to stay functional: she catalogs. She missed the signal — the attentiveness, the preparation she read wrong. She did not know. Not knowing means she did not have the last honest conversation with him. She will carry that. It is hers now; she does not know what else to do with it.

She enters the secondary code — the date reversed. The partition opens.

The documentation package is large. She starts at the broadcast architecture file.

She reads for six minutes.

When she looks up, the quality of the light through the high window has shifted. Later morning. The quay is starting to move.

OTTO
There's a train east at fourteen hundred.

NADIA
I need the room until then.

He nods.

OTTO
He was a careful man.

He says it not as comfort. He says it as a fact he has been holding, and he has now given it to someone who needed to have it.

NADIA
Yes.

She goes back to the documentation.
