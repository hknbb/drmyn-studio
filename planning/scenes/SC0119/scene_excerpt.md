Okafor stands. Tobias does not. He is looking at the instruments again — not arranging them now, just looking at them.

TOBIAS
She got him out.

Not a question.

OKAFOR
Yes.

TOBIAS
Good.

He picks up the penlight again. Clicks it once. The room holds the sound.

He does not say anything else. There is nothing else to say that is his to say.


INT. JOINT LEGAL OPERATIONS CENTER — EVENING

The room is quieter than it was at fourteen hundred. Not empty — Chen Mei is at her station, secondary tranche processing; two analysts remain at the far monitors — but the acute operational tempo has passed. What replaces it is the slower rhythm of documentation, the work of converting events into record.

Okafor stands at the central display. She has been standing there for three minutes.

STATE DIGEST / 18:47 / CASE: ORACLE PRIME NETWORK
ARRESTS CONFIRMED: 14 / PENDING: 7
ACCOUNTS FROZEN: 847 / JURISDICTIONS: 3
EVIDENTIARY SUBMISSIONS: 94 (PRIMARY) + 43 (SECONDARY, IN PROGRESS)
NAMED DEFENDANTS: 31 / UNNAMED PERSONS OF INTEREST: 112+

---

She reads the line that reads 112+.

The plus is because the count is incomplete. It will stay incomplete. Some of those 112 will be identified over the course of the trials. Some will not be. Some will be identified and the evidence will not be sufficient. Some will be identified and sufficient and will appeal for years, and the appeals will succeed on procedural grounds, and the cases will be refiled, and the appeals will happen again. She has been doing this work for nineteen years. She knows what the plus means.

It means: not all of them.

She does not write this down. It is not the kind of thing you write down. It is the kind of thing you carry.

---

Chen Mei looks up from her station.

CHEN MEI
Secondary tranche — forty-three submissions. Filed and confirmed. Jurisdiction two is requesting the supplemental ledger pages.

OKAFOR
Send them.

Chen Mei sends them. The room continues. One of the analysts at the far monitors notes a timestamp, enters a record. The documentation work of a case that will run for years has begun and is doing what it does: accumulating.

---

Okafor closes the state digest. The display returns to the operational queue — the next tranche, the pending arrest confirmations, the supplemental requests from three jurisdictions that will each have their own timeline and their own procedural requirements and their own pace.

She looks at it.

OKAFOR
It's enough to begin.

Not to Chen Mei. Not to the room. To the case.

Chen Mei hears it. Does not respond. There is nothing to add to a thing that accurate.

The work continues.


INT. SAFE HOUSE — A ROOM — MORNING

A table. A lamp. Three folders, two envelopes, a set of photographs, a list of biographical details typed on plain paper. The materials for becoming someone.

She works through them in order.
