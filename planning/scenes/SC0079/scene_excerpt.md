INT. KASPAR TERMINAL — STORAGE CORRIDOR — NIGHT

The corridor is not on the broadcast grid. Nadia identified this gap seven hours ago and has not used it until now. She uses it now.

Zara is already here.

She is standing with her back against the shelving, her hands at her sides. Watching the door. She knew Nadia would come. The only question was when.

NADIA
I need VELTAIN access. You have the route credentials.

ZARA
I have one set. They expire at the next credential cycle. That gives you — us — twenty hours.

NADIA
That's enough.

ZARA
Tell me why I give them to you.

A beat. Nadia does not look away.

NADIA
Because what I'm offering you is better than what Roman is paying you to hunt me.

ZARA
Roman is paying me to hunt you. What you're offering is a story. I don't take stories.

NADIA
It's not a story. It's a name and a confirmation.

ZARA
Go.

NADIA
Inspector Okafor. Cross-jurisdictional financial crimes unit. She is currently in receipt of a documentation chain that contains Dimitri Koss's operational ledger. The ledger includes the construction of a fabricated legal liability — the mechanism Roman used to put you in the hunter pool. It is timestamped. It has provenance. It is already with a legal officer who is not compromised and cannot be bought.

A long pause. Zara's hands are very still. Her eyes move.

ZARA
You're telling me someone is dismantling the debt.

NADIA
I'm telling you it's already in motion. I didn't start it. A man named Marcus started it forty-one weeks ago. I am telling you it exists because you need to know it exists, and because if I don't come back from VELTAIN, it keeps moving without me.

ZARA
The debt is —

She stops. Starts again.

ZARA (CONT'D)
The liability is fabricated. I knew that. I couldn't prove it and I couldn't pay it and every lawyer I found told me —

She stops again. Her sentence does not complete. Nadia does not help it.

NADIA
Dimitri documented it. The ledger has the construction. Date, method, the chain of signatures. Okafor will have everything she needs to dissolve it.

A silence. Zara's gaze drops to mid-distance for a moment — not at Nadia, not at the corridor. Then back.

ZARA
How do I verify Okafor is real.

NADIA
Her name, rank, and unit are public record. Her current posting is documented. You can check in four minutes on any device that isn't inside this building's broadcast network.

ZARA
And if she doesn't move on it.

NADIA
The chain has two copies in two locations. One is already with her. One remains in separate custody. If Okafor is blocked or compromised, the second copy activates a different channel. The chain was built to survive interference.

ZARA
By Marcus.

NADIA
Yes.

Another silence. Zara looks at the shelving beside her. Straightens a container that does not need straightening. She is very busy for three seconds with something that requires no attention.

ZARA
What do you need from me exactly. At VELTAIN.

NADIA
Route access. Your credentials at the outer perimeter. What you know about the internal layout from prior operations.

ZARA
I've been inside VELTAIN twice. I know the east approach and the lower service level. I don't know the executive tier.

NADIA
I don't need the executive tier. I need to reach the residential compound.

ZARA
Jin.

NADIA
Yes.

ZARA
You know he may not be — that Roman's people may have already —

NADIA
I know.

Zara exhales once, audibly, through her nose.

ZARA
My terms. If Lena is clear before VELTAIN — if I get confirmation the liability is dissolved before we go in — I stay through the compound. If I don't get confirmation, I get you to the perimeter and no further. I'm not going to die for a promise.

NADIA
Agreed.

ZARA
And if this goes wrong and Okafor doesn't move —

NADIA
Then the debt stays. I know. That's the risk.

ZARA
That's your risk. It's my daughter.

NADIA
Yes. It is.

The two women look at each other. No pretense between them. No warmth either. This is what an alliance looks like before it has been tested: two people with overlapping problems and the mutual recognition that the overlap is real.

ZARA
If this falls apart — if you're using me to get to VELTAIN and the rest is leverage —

NADIA
Then you'll know I lied and you'll have learned something. But I'm not lying.

Zara exhales again, shorter this time. A different sound — not acceptance of a cost, but the end of deliberation.

ZARA
Tell me the route.
