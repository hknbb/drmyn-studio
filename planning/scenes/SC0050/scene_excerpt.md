INT. MERIN — QUAY DISTRICT, UNIT 14-B — LATE MORNING

She is standing, checking the note she wrote — 7-E — against what she has in Marcus's documentation. Cross-referencing. Making the single location real.

The signal unit activates.

OTTO looks at it. He does not reach for it. He looks at NADIA.

She picks up.

TOBIAS (V.O.)
There's something you need to know before you go near any of it.

He cut the first call eighteen minutes ago. He called back anyway.

NADIA
Go ahead.

TOBIAS (V.O.)
What you're looking at — ORACLE PRIME — it's not what it looks like from the outside. The broadcast is real. The hunt is real. But the broadcast is also a routing shell. The money that moves through the betting architecture isn't gambling revenue. It's six networks running criminal capital through the event's financial layer. The gambling platform is the surface. Underneath it: a financial routing system.

She is still.

TOBIAS (V.O.) (CONT'D)
I've been treating the enforcement layer's personnel for two years. They talk when they're injured. I've heard enough. It's not a game operation that also moves money. It's a money-moving operation that also runs a game. The game is the cover story.

The scale of what she has been surviving is not what she thought it was. The machine she was prepared to dismantle is the visible surface of something that has six other owners.

She sits down.

She does not speak for a moment. This is not hesitation. This is the accounting that has to happen before anything else.

Then:

NADIA
What is the one thing that stops it?

Not the hunt. The machine.

A pause on the line — brief. He already knows the answer. He is deciding whether to give it.

TOBIAS (V.O.)
The broadcast architecture. The money can't move without it — the broadcast is the routing shell. The financial layer runs through the same infrastructure as the game. Take down the broadcast, the routing collapses. I've heard it more than once from people who would know.

She processes this.

It is the same answer Marcus left in the documentation. The same target. Different source, different method, same answer.

NADIA
Thank you.

TOBIAS (V.O.)
Don't.

The line ends.

OTTO has not moved from the window. The quay is moving outside. The morning is later than it was.

She sets down the relay unit.

The broadcast architecture is not a kill switch for a hunt. It is the kill switch for the financial infrastructure of six criminal organizations. The stakeholders are not limited to Roman. The exposure, the risk, the consequences of success — all of it is proportionally larger than she had calculated.

She knows this. She has the target.

She picks up her note — 7-E — and folds it into her jacket pocket.

OTTO
Train's in ninety minutes.

NADIA
I know.

She moves.
