INT. MERIN — QUAY DISTRICT, UNIT 14-B — LATE MORNING

The documentation is closed. The partition is locked.

She has everything Marcus could give her. She doesn't have a route in.

NADIA
Was there anyone else Marcus trusted inside the network?

OTTO does not answer immediately. He has been at the window the entire time. He turns now — not toward her, toward a point somewhere between her and the door.

OTTO
One.

NADIA
Name.

OTTO
Tobias Orell. He runs a practice off the MERIN freight grid. Underground. Marcus used him twice. They knew each other — not just operational. More than that.

She processes this. The name is not unknown to her.

OTTO (CONT'D)
I don't know if he'll pick up. The channel goes through three relays. If he thinks the call is compromised he'll drop it without answering.

He crosses to the equipment — a small signal unit mounted under the table, institutional-grey, the kind that is not sold. He pulls a frequency card from a locked case, sets it in front of her.

OTTO (CONT'D)
His node is the third relay. Don't call from your own line. Use this.

She picks up the card. Studies it for three seconds. Then she sits at the table and dials the relay chain.

A delay. The kind that is not latency but distance — three relays routing through points that do not appear on any carrier map.

OTTO moves to the far end of the room again. She is alone in the call.

A click. Then silence that is not dead — it is monitored.

TOBIAS (V.O.)
— Go ahead.

Not a greeting. An instruction.

NADIA
Tobias. I came through OTTO. I was connected to Marcus Chen.

A pause. She can hear him deciding.

TOBIAS (V.O.)
I know who you are.

Of course he does. The game has been live for days.

NADIA
I'm not asking you for anything that puts you in the event. One question. Specific.

TOBIAS (V.O.)
You understand there's a cost to this.

NADIA
I understand.

TOBIAS (V.O.)
Ask.

NADIA
Roman's enforcement assets in MERIN — local staging. Physical location. Fixed or rotating.

Another pause. This one is longer. She can hear him running the calculation — what he knows, what it costs to say it, who is listening to this relay that he thinks he controls but cannot be certain he controls.

TOBIAS (V.O.)
Fixed. Freight zone — Unit 7-Echo. I've seen the same vehicles serviced there three times in fourteen days. Same crew, same schedule window. Early morning.

NADIA
That's all I needed.

TOBIAS (V.O.)
Then we're done.

The line cuts. Not rudely — cleanly, the way someone cuts a call when they know the open time has just passed its limit.

NADIA sets down the relay unit.

OTTO has not moved.

She does not say what she is thinking, which is that she just put Tobias Orell into the sightline of a machine that has a perfect record, and he answered her question anyway. She knew this before she dialed. He knew it before he answered. Neither of them said it.

She writes: 7-E. Three words. Then she folds the note.

OTTO
He was careful too. Marcus. He said Tobias was good people.

She stands.

NADIA
Where is the 7-Echo unit from here?

OTTO
Four kilometers. Freight access road, not public transit.

She nods. She has what she needs and now she has a direction.
