## Excerpt

```fountain
Roman remains. He picks up the glass of water — the first time he has touched it — and drinks.

He sets it down. Looks at the door.

He gave Dimitri the scope and the methodology and the timeline. He gave him the list — twelve names, eleven of which are misdirection. The twelfth is a test of where Dimitri's independent attention goes.

He has done what he needed to do.

He is not certain it will be enough.


INT. ANONYMOUS ROOM — NIGHT

Later than usual. The kind of hour that exists outside the day's accounting.

MARCUS lies on his back, looking at the ceiling. Not sleepless — he has the stillness of a man who has been awake for a while and decided not to fight it.

NADIA is beside him. She is awake too. She knows he is awake. Neither has said so.

The room is quiet in the specific way of a room in a city — the sound outside is continuous and therefore absent.

MARCUS
Tell me something I don't know.

NADIA
You know everything.

MARCUS
I know a great deal. Not everything.

She thinks for a moment. Not performing thought — actually searching for something she hasn't said.

NADIA
My father called Vardova the city that practiced forgetting. He said every city has something it specializes in. Warsaw remembers. Prague mourns. Vardova forgets.

MARCUS
Does he still say that.

NADIA
He died four years ago.

A beat.

MARCUS
I didn't know that.

NADIA
You do now.

He turns his head to look at her. She is looking at the ceiling. This is, without comment, one of the small satisfactions of the thing between them — that she has given him something true.

---

Later.

The room has shifted — the light has changed, or the silence has, or some interior thing. They are in the aftermath of a different conversation, one the scene has not shown.

Marcus sits on the edge of the bed. He is looking at his hands. Nadia watches him.

She has been watching him the way she watches things she is trying to understand — not intrusively, quietly, the way a person reads.

He carries something tonight. She has known it since he arrived. She has accepted the surface because the surface is what he is offering and she will not take more than she is offered.

MARCUS
(without looking at her)
Merin old quay. Fourteen-B. In case you lost the paper.

He says it the way a person gives a reminder. Not a warning. The address in his voice is the same address on the paper — the same precision, the same evenness.

Nadia looks at him.

NADIA
I haven't lost it.

MARCUS
Good.

He nods once. The subject closes.

She does not ask what he means by in case. She does not ask what he knows that he is not saying. She does not ask why the address needs to exist at all — why redundancy, why now, why twice.

She does not ask because asking would open something, and she has made the decision — not consciously, the way these decisions are actually made — to protect the night from what is outside it.
```
