## Excerpt

```fountain
He lies back down. His arm comes around her. She puts her hand on his chest.

His breathing slows and steadies. He is, or appears to be, asleep.

She is not asleep.

She stays still and listens to the city outside and does not think about the address. She thinks about his father's betting parlor, which he told her about once, and his specific relationship to numbers — that he trusts them and has never been entirely at ease with the trust. She thinks about the scar on his right index finger.

She thinks about these things because they are his, and she is in the habit of holding them.

She does not think: this is the last time.

But she stays awake longer than she needs to.


INT. ROMAN'S TROPHY ROOM — VALE RESIDENCE — NIGHT

The room referenced in conversation at the SC0005 party. Now seen fully for the first time.

Objects on shelves. Items under glass. A room of careful curation — not displays of wealth, displays of outcome. Each object has a provenance. Each one is evidence of a judgment Roman made and the judgment was correct.

ROMAN stands with a phone in his hand, looking at a document. He has been standing this way for approximately ninety seconds, which is a long time for Roman.

The document is not shown in full. What is visible: a name, a date, a laboratory reference number. The confirmation he paid for and waited for and did not want to need.

He sets the document on the shelf beside him. Not filing it away — setting it down, the way you set something down when you need both hands free and your hands are already free.

He looks at the room.

---

He moves along the shelves. Not pacing — this is deliberate. He stops at a specific object: a small glass case, something inside it that we cannot identify from this angle. He looks at it for a moment.

Something passes across his face.

Not grief — grief is a room you enter and close the door behind you. This is something that moves across the surface and is gone before it can become anything.

He looks away from the case.

---

He dials.

Two rings.

DIMITRI (V.O.)
Yes.

ROMAN
The source confirmed.

A silence on the line. Dimitri processing.

DIMITRI (V.O.)
Timeline.

ROMAN
Immediate.

Another pause.

DIMITRI (V.O.)
The timing is suboptimal. There are three external observers with active visibility windows in the next —

ROMAN
Prepare.

Silence.

DIMITRI (V.O.)
Understood.

The call ends.
```
