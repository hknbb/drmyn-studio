## Shot Purpose

Cover the shift from the Roman office carry-over beat to the Marcus/Nadia night scene in
the anonymous room. The motion target is the contrast between the tense carry-over
atmosphere (subsiding) and the private, low-motion bed space. The motion must allow a
ceiling-facing two-shot at rest and the later edge-of-bed separation as Marcus hands over
the address.

## Motion Description

Low-motion scene. The camera holds a frame wide enough for the ceiling two-shot, then
allows the intimacy of the edge-of-bed shift without cutting aggressively. Negative space
is load-bearing (CG07) — do not fill it. The motion of the scene lives in Marcus's shift
to the bed edge and his arm withdrawal, not in camera movement.
