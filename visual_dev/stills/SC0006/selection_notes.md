## Selection Target

When stills are generated, priority candidates are:

1. Study environment establishing frame — desk, shelves, grille axis, controlled night
   interior. Nadia in the room before the discovery beat; pre-awareness register.
2. The vent / grille detail — the lens edge must be legible without being foregrounded
   as spectacle. Low-key selective definition is the target; enough to read, not enough
   to editorialize.
3. Nadia's sightline from desk or shelf toward the grille — the calculation the camera
   must make legible (CG06). The spatial geometry should read.
4. Nadia post-discovery stillness — composure intact, the variable priced. Nothing in
   her posture reveals that anything has changed. That is the scene's point.

Tonal register: muted study neutrals, dark shelf mass, controlled night interior. Low-key
enough to read the vent wire and lens edge, not so dark that the spatial relations are
lost.
