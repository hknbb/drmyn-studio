# Validation Statement

DRMYN Studio is validated as a metadata-only, schema-validated, human-gated workflow for AI-assisted movie development and movie production process design.

Hakan Babacan primarily created the software, repository architecture, metadata workflow, validation structure, and release package.

Assoc. Prof. Dr. Gizem Parlayandemir Sayan performed cinema studies and social-science validation, conceptual review, domain evaluation, and manuscript correction. Her contribution focused on the disciplinary validity, interpretive framing, and cinema/social-science relevance of the movie development and movie production process model.

Ra. Dr. Serra Çelik performed technical-scientific validation, computer science review, software correction, methodological verification, and reproducibility-oriented checking. Her contribution focused on the computational, methodological, and technical correctness of the software and the article's computer science sections.

All human authors reviewed and approved the final manuscript.
